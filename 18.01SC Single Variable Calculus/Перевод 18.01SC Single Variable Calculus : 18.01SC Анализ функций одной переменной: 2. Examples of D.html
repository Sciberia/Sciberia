We start by computing the slope of the secant line:
$$
\begin{align}
\frac{\Delta f}{\Delta x} &= \frac{f(x_0 + \Delta x) − f(x_0)}{\Delta x} \\
&= \frac{\frac{1}{x_0+ \Delta x_0} - \frac{1}{x_0}}{\Delta x} \\
&= \frac{(x_0)(x_0 + \Delta x)}{(x_0)(x_0 + \Delta x)} \frac{\frac{1}{x_0 + \Delta x} - \frac{1}{x_0}}{\Delta x} \\
&= \frac{\frac{(x_0)(x_0 + \Delta x)}{x_0 + \Delta x} - \frac{(x_0)(x_0 + \Delta x)}{\Delta x}}{(x_0)(x_0 + \Delta x) \Delta x)} \\
&= \frac{1}{\Delta x} \frac{x_0 - (x_0 + \Delta x)}{(x_0)(x_0 + \Delta x)} \\
&=\frac{1}{\Delta x} \frac{-\Delta x}{(x_0)(x_0 + \Delta x)} \\
&=\frac{-1}{(x_0)(x_0 + \Delta x)}
\end{align}
$$

#6

Начнем с вычисления угла наклона секущей:
$$
\begin{align}
\frac{\Delta f}{\Delta x} &= \frac{f(x_0 + \Delta x) − f(x_0)}{\Delta x} \\[6pt]
&= \frac{\frac{1}{x_0+ \Delta x_0} - \frac{1}{x_0}}{\Delta x} \\[6pt]
&= \frac{(x_0)(x_0 + \Delta x)}{(x_0)(x_0 + \Delta x)} \frac{\frac{1}{x_0 + \Delta x} - \frac{1}{x_0}}{\Delta x} \\[6pt]
&= \frac{\frac{(x_0)(x_0 + \Delta x)}{x_0 + \Delta x} - \frac{(x_0)(x_0 + \Delta x)}{x_0}}{(x_0)(x_0 + \Delta x) \Delta x)} \\[6pt]
&= \frac{1}{\Delta x} \frac{x_0 - (x_0 + \Delta x)}{(x_0)(x_0 + \Delta x)} \\[6pt]
&=\frac{1}{\Delta x} \frac{-\Delta x}{(x_0)(x_0 + \Delta x)} \\[6pt]
&=\frac{-1}{(x_0)(x_0 + \Delta x)}
\end{align}
$$

We’ve computed that $f'(x) = \frac{−1}{{x_0}^2}$. Is this correct? How might we check our work? First of all, $f'(x_0)$ is negative — as is the slope of the tangent line on the graph of $y = \frac{1}{x}$. Secondly, as $x_0 \to \infty$ (i.e. as $x_0$ grows larger and larger), the tangent line is less and less steep. So $\frac{1}{{x_0}^2}$ should get closer to $0$ as $x_0$ increases, which it does.

#10

Мы посчитали производную $f'(x) = \frac{−1}{{x_0}^2}$. Правильно ли это? Как мы можем это проверить? Во вторых, если $x_0 \to \infty$ (то есть $x_0$ становится все больше и больше), наклон касательной становится все менее и менее крутым. Так что, при возрастании $x_0$, значение $\frac{1}{{x_0}^2}$ должно становится все ближе к $0$, как это и происходит.

+0

To get the common denominator, we multiply the first fraction by $\frac{(x_0)}{(x_0)} = 1$ and the second by $\frac{(x_0 + \Delta x)}{(x_0 + \Delta x)}$. When we said $y = \frac{1}{x}$, we were thinking of $y$ as the vertical position of a point on the hyperbola. When we said $y − y_0 = \frac{−1}{{x_0}^2} (x − x_0)$ we were thinking of $y$ as the vertical position of a point on the tangent line.
